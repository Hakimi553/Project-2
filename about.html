About Us

HELLO, FROM KOPPIKU

We are a home-grown coffee company from Malaysia that aims to make premium coffee accessible to all. From beans to cups, we deliver on affordability, without compromising on quality.


Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring exceptional coffee experiences to every corner of our community. We source our beans directly from local farmers, ensuring fair trade practices and the freshest quality for our customers.


At Koppiku, we believe that great coffee should be an everyday luxury, not a rare treat. That's why we've crafted our menu to offer premium beverages and artisanal food at prices that won't break the bank.


Whether you're looking for your morning energy boost, an afternoon pick-me-up, or a cozy evening treat, we're here to serve you with passion, quality, and a warm smile.
